Exploring & visualizing HRSA grant data with a Tableau story

The main tasks:
  • Explore and summarize data with python/pandas (via Jupyter notebook)
  • Import the dataset into Tableau and build visuals
  • Organize the visuals into logical and compelling dashboards


Dashboards:
  • Overview of Grant Data


  • Trends by Region


  • A Deep Dive into the Grantees


  • Explore Data for Your State




Notable Trends:
  • Region IV (Southeastern states) received the highest amount of funds, compared to the other regions. However, California has recieved a significantly higher amount of HRSA funds, compared to other individual states.

  • The amount of HRSA grant funds awarded displays a growing trend, with "Primary Health" and "HIV/AIDS" related programs comprising the majority.





This Tableau story provides insight into which locations, programs, and entities are more likely to recieve health-related grant funds.
